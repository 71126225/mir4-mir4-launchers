Screen composition / Key Description / Commands / Actions and Behaviors

1. Use the store
  • Simply go to the store you want and use the command below.
    • Selling Items: "Sell Meat" or "Sell 2 Meats"
                      You can also click on the item you want to sell in your inventory and use the "Sell" key.
    • Buying Items: "Buy Meat" or "Buy 2 Meats"
                     Right-click on a clerk in a store to bring up a menu. Click on the item you want, enter the quantity, and then buy it.
    • Store your stuff: "Take the meat" or "Take 2 meats"
                     This is the ability to store your stuff.
    • Find Items: "Give me back the meat" or "Give me back 2 pieces of meat"
                     This is a function to find items that you have stored.
    • View Menu: Say "What's up?" to see the store's menu.
    • View Quantity: You can see how many items are being sold in the "How many meats" store.
    • View Stock: "Meat" allows you to see the number of specific items you have in your stock.
    • Shayang View: "What's that meat?" You can find out the specifications of a specific item.
                     You can also click on an item in your bag and use the "Info" key to view its specifications.
    • Send an item: "Send an item" A function that allows you to send an item that you are storing to another person..
                     The item must be stored and there is a fee of 5,000 won.
                     Example) "Please give me meat" (If you use this, the following menu will appear.)
                     1. Who would you like to send it to? (Recipient's name)
                     2. What would you like to send? (Name of item to send)
                     3. How much would you like to send? (number to send)
                     4. Would you like to send X XX to XX? (Yes/No)
                     If you want to cancel due to a typo or other reason during sending, just type "Cancel."


2.Exchange items

  • Added feature to prevent item fraud. When facing the character you want to exchange items with, use the command below.
    • Example) @Exchange meat 1==>Dumpling


3.Lord's Command

* The lord's functions are to issue orders only within his own castle..
* When a castle is conquered, the public funds that have been accumulated during that time become the next castle possession.
  • Set tax rates: A feature that allows taxes to be withheld when purchasing goods in the city.
    • Example) @Tax rate 10% (adjustable from 0 to 50%)
  • Deposit: The ability to deposit money to yourself, or to your private property and clan in times of emergency.
    • Example) @Deposit 1,000,000 gold
  • Withdraw: You can use the supply on the top.
    • Example) @Withdrawal 1,000,000 gold
  • Release: You can release a person who is incarcerated in your castle prison.
    • Example) @Release Mir's Legend
  • Wanted: You can place a wanted notice on any resident.
    • Example) @Wanted Devil Kiss 1 (1,000,000 gold per hour/from public treasury)
